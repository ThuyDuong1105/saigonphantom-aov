Saigon Phantoms a professional Arena of Valor team based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, competing in the AOG league. Known as the league's longest-standing team, they have consistently placed in the Top 4 over 14 seasons. SGP holds titles like APL 2023 champion, AIC 2020 runner-up, and a record nine AOG championships. In 2022, their roster represented Vietnam in Arena of Valor at the 31st SEA Games in Hanoi.
